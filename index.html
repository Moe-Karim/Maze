ABOUT THE GAME

In a mysterious maze, three characters embark on an adventure. The Explorer seeks treasures, using instincts to find the way. The Hazmat worker protects everyone from hidden dangers. The Tryhard faces every challenge with determination. Together, they uncover secrets and learn the power of teamwork.

EXPLORER

The Explorer loves to find hidden treasures in the maze. They carry a backpack and have a good sense of direction. Every time they discover something new, they get closer to solving the maze's mysteries.

HAZMAT

The Hazmat worker wears a special suit to explore the maze's dangers. They use special tools to check for harmful substances and collect samples. Their goal is to understand the problems in the maze and keep the outside world safe.

TRYHARD

The Tryhard never gives up and wants to beat every challenge in the maze. With each level, they work hard and use all their skills to win. They encourage others to keep trying, showing that hard work can lead to success.
